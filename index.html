jsPhotoCradle

An elegant wrap of your photo collection.

What is this?

Did you ever wish to show photos you store on Ð° service like Flickr or Picasa on your personal website or blog? jsPhotoCradle is designed to deliver photos from 3rd party services to your website and wrap it into a smooth customizable slideshow.

How it works?

jsPhotoCradle is a client based javascript driven application. It uses popular photo services APIs to get URL addresses of specific photos and builds dynamic CSS customizable slideshow.
